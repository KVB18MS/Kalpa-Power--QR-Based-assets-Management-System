Design Elements

The icon design incorporates several elements that represent the Kalpa Power brand and app functionality:

  • Green Background (#4CAF50): Represents the eco-friendly, sustainable nature of solar power
  • Solar Panel: The main design element showcasing the company's solar focus
  • Sun Rays: Symbolizes solar energy and power generation
  • QR Code Element: Represents the app's core inventory management functionality

Source Files

The original SVG source files are included in the zip package for future modifications:

  • icon_template.svg - Standard icon template
  • icon_template_round.svg - Round icon template

Implementation Notes

To use these icons in your Android project:

  1. Copy the mipmap folders to your project's res directory
  2. Ensure your AndroidManifest.xml has the proper references:
android:icon="@mipmap/ic_launcher"
android:roundIcon="@mipmap/ic_launcher_round"

These icons follow Android's recommended sizing guidelines for launcher icons across all standard density buckets.
